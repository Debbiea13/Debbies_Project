1.1 Describing the purpose of IT in modern business

The purpose of Information Technology in modern business is to store, retrieve, transmit and manipulate data or information. From small businesses to multi-national corporations, IT has become one of the most important tools that is used in every department.

Email is one of the main communications between employees, suppliers and customers with other tools evolving that give staff instant communication - using live chat, video-conferencing and Smart phone apps. All these tools are quicker and are an efficient way of ordering supplies, giving customer support and out of hours questions and answers.

Websites can reach a larger audience without the cost of representatives flying halfway around the world to engage with potential customers. Surveys can be completed online, advertising banners on Facebook can give you a true figure on how many people are interested in your product.

Working Away has never been easier, with remote access to your company server productivity is increased.

Record Keeping that needs to be kept for a long time can be kept on a server without taking up large amounts of space like storing paper versions. Sensitive files can be encrypted adding security to data. [1] [2] [3]
